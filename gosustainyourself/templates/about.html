Our Mission

Go Sustain Yourself was created to bridge the gap between environmental awareness and actionable sustainability practices. Our platform combines machine learning with practical tools to help users understand and reduce their ecological footprint.

Our Features

Carbon Footprint Calculator

Our advanced algorithm analyzes your lifestyle choices to estimate your carbon emissions and provide personalized reduction strategies.

Recyclability Classifier

Upload images of items to instantly determine if they're recyclable and learn proper disposal methods.

Sustainability Resources

Access our curated database of eco-friendly tips, local recycling centers, and sustainable product alternatives.

The Technology

Our platform leverages:

  • Django for robust backend functionality
  • Google's Gemini AI for intelligent suggestions
  • Computer vision for image recognition
  • Geolocation APIs for local recycling resources
  • Five Machine Learning model with combined of 95%+ accuracy
  • Responsive Bootstrap design for all devices
